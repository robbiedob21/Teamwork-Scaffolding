Our Services

Supply & Erect

Our versatile tube and coupler systems offer unmatched flexibility, adapting to any project size, shape, and complexity.

Our experienced erection team will safely and efficiently assemble your scaffold, adhering to all relevant WorkSafe NZ regulations.

We offer a variety of scaffold heights, configurations, and access points to meet your specific needs.

Scaffold Design & Engineering

Need a custom-designed scaffold for a unique project? Our certified engineers are here to help.

We can design complex structures for challenging projects, including roof structures for shrink wrap protection, intricate building shapes, and uneven terrain.

Our designs adhere to the strictest safety standards and ensure optimal functionality for your specific needs.

Contract Labour

Do you have the equipment but need experienced hands to assemble your scaffold? Our team of certified scaffolders is ready to assist.

We ensure all our scaffolders are WorkSafe NZ compliant and possess the necessary skills and knowledge to build safe and stable structures.

Whether you need a full crew for a large project or just a helping hand, we can provide the perfect solution.

Equipment Hire

Don't need a full scaffold setup? We offer a wide range of equipment for rent, allowing you to tackle smaller projects or specific access needs.

Our rental inventory includes scaffold planks, access staircases, propping equipment, mobile towers, and aluminum bridging beams.

We offer flexible rental periods and competitive rates to suit your project timeline and budget.
